
Hall - Basic clan private space, may contain sub-sections

	Common domestic subdivisions

	Dwelling - individual family living space, generally for married couples only
	Chreech - area for small children are kept
	Warren - a group dormitory for adults/guests
	Dinning - Meal areas
	Meeting - political gatherings if separate from dining
	Privy
	
Workshop - root working areas

	Smithy, Masonry, Carpentry, Machinery, Pottery
	
Governmental

	Dungeon - prisoner holding
	Guard Post - station for guards
	Treasury - treasure hoarding
	Training Ground - military practice
